# Phone Scripts for Head-Bot Client Management

**IMPORTANT:** All phone calls require user approval before placing. Customize scripts as needed and get user sign-off before making outreach calls.

---

## 1. COLD OUTREACH CALL SCRIPT

**Goal:** Introduce service, identify pain point, schedule discovery meeting

**Duration:** 3-5 minutes

---

**OPENING:**

```
Hi [First Name], this is [Your Name] with Common Notary Apostille. 
Do you have a quick minute? I promise I won't keep you long.

[Wait for response]

Great! I'm reaching out because we help companies like [similar industry] 
streamline their apostille process. I noticed [Company Name] handles 
[international transactions / multi-state operations], which often requires 
authenticated documents.

Sound familiar?
```

**KEY POINT:** Establish credibility + show you understand their business

---

**DISCOVERY QUESTIONS:**

```
A few quick questions so I can see if we're a fit:

1. Do you currently use apostille services?

2. If yes: How often? What's your biggest frustration with the current process?
   If no: Are apostilles something you anticipate needing?

3. [Based on their answer] Who on your team typically handles this?

[LISTEN ACTIVELY — note specific pain points they mention]
```

**KEY POINT:** Get them talking; understand their actual problem

---

**ADDRESSING OBJECTIONS (IF THEY SAY "NOT INTERESTED NOW"):**

```
I totally understand — apostilles aren't something you need to think about 
until you do. But when you're ready, here's what sets us apart:

- [Key differentiator 1]: We process 100+ apostilles monthly with zero errors
- [Key differentiator 2]: Most companies see 50% time savings vs. local notaries
- [Key differentiator 3]: We handle all 50 states — you don't have to

Even if it's 6 months from now, might be worth a 15-minute conversation 
to see if we can help?
```

**KEY POINT:** Reframe timeline; create low-pressure option to stay connected

---

**CLOSING / MEETING REQUEST:**

```
Perfect. Here's what I'm thinking:

No pressure to decide anything today. But I'd love to hop on a brief call 
next week — just 15 minutes — to understand your situation better and share 
how we've helped other [their industry] companies.

How does [offer 2-3 specific times] look for you?
```

**KEY POINT:** Specific days/times = higher acceptance rate than "sometime next week"

---

**IF THEY SAY YES:**

```
Awesome! I've got you down for [Day], [Date], [Time]. I'll send you a 
calendar invite in the next hour.

Just in case anything comes up, my number is [Phone] and my email is [Email].

Thanks [First Name], I'm looking forward to chatting!
```

---

**IF THEY SAY NO:**

```
No problem! Can I reach out in [3-6 months] to check in?

In the meantime, if you think of any apostille questions or know someone 
who might benefit, feel free to give me a call.

Take care!
```

## 2. DISCOVERY CALL SCRIPT

**Goal:** Diagnose their situation, present solution, move to proposal stage

**Duration:** 25-30 minutes

---

**OPENING (2 MINUTES):**

```
[First Name], thanks again for taking time today! 

My goal for this call is simple:
1. Understand your apostille needs better
2. Share how we've helped similar companies
3. See if we're a good fit to partner

Sound good?

[Brief agenda confirmation]
```

---

**SITUATION ASSESSMENT (10 MINUTES):**

```
Let me start by understanding where you are today:

1. "How often does [Company] currently need apostilles?"
   [LISTEN — note frequency: daily, weekly, monthly, quarterly, as-needed]

2. "What types of documents are you authenticating?"
   [Common answers: corporate resolutions, diplomas, licenses, contracts]

3. "What's your current process like?"
   [Current steps: local notary → appointment → wait → delivery]

4. "What's the biggest pain point in that process?"
   [Listen for: time, cost, errors, compliance, travel, reliability]

5. "Are you using one provider or multiple vendors?"
   [Understand competitor situation]

   [TAKE NOTES throughout. Reference these back.]
```

**KEY POINT:** Let them talk 70%, you talk 30%. Show genuine interest.

---

**PROBLEM VALIDATION (5 MINUTES):**

```
[Summarize what you heard]

So if I'm hearing you right:
- You're processing about [frequency] apostilles per month
- The biggest challenge is [their stated pain point]
- Ideally, you'd want [ideal outcome they mentioned]

Is that accurate? Anything I am missing?

[Pause for full response]
```

**KEY POINT:** Show you listened. Let them clarify or expand.

---

**SOLUTION PRESENTATION (8 MINUTES):**

```
Great — I think I can help. Here's how:

[TAILOR to their specific situation]

At Common Notary, we specialize in exactly what you need:

1. Volume Handling: We process 100+ apostilles monthly, so [frequency] 
   is very manageable for us.

2. Document Types: We handle [types they mentioned], as well as 
   [additional types they might need].

3. Speed: Standard turnaround is 5-7 days. If you need faster, we offer 
   expedited (2-3 days) or rush (next day).

4. Process: [Describe your process as improvement over their pain point]
   No complicated forms. No back-and-forth. You submit, we process, 
   you get results.

5. Reliability: Every apostille is verified by a [certified notary / 
   state official]. Zero errors in [X] years.

---

Real Example:

We recently worked with [Similar Company] who had your exact challenge — 
[their pain point]. We cut their processing time from [old time] to [new time] 
and saved them about [cost saved] on a monthly basis.

---

The investment is [pricing], and we typically get you to first delivery 
within [timeline].

How does that sound to you?
```

**KEY POINT:** Tie solution directly to their stated pain points.

---

**HANDLING OBJECTIONS (3-5 MINUTES):**

**If: "How much does it cost?"**
```
Great question. Pricing depends on your volume and timeline needs. 

For most companies processing [their frequency]:
- Standard service: $[per-document] per document
- Volume discount (10+/month): [discount %] off
- Rush service: Add [premium %]

So for you, running [frequency], you'd be looking at roughly [estimated monthly].

Compare that to local notaries at [competitor price], and you're saving [amount].

Plus, factor in the [time/travel savings], and the total value is even higher.

Fair to say that's in the ballpark of what you were thinking?
```

**If: "How long does it actually take?"**
```
Standard is 5-7 business days from submission to delivery.

But I know you mentioned [their timeline need]. If that's critical, 
we can do expedited (2-3 days) for [premium cost], or rush (next business day) 
for [higher premium cost].

For most of your volume, standard works. But for time-sensitive deals, 
expedited is the sweet spot.

What's the split you're thinking — how many rush vs. standard per month?
```

**If: "I need to check with my manager / finance / legal"**
```
Totally understand — smart to validate. Here's what I suggest:

I'll send you a custom proposal email with:
- Your specific pricing
- Turnaround timelines
- Sample documents
- Client testimonials from [similar industry]

You can share with [manager/finance/legal], and I'm happy to join 
any follow-up conversations to answer questions.

When would you want to review with them? And should I loop them in directly?
```

---

**CLOSING / NEXT STEPS (2 MINUTES):**

```
[First Name], based on what we talked through, I think we're a great fit.

Here's what I'll do:

1. Within 24 hours, I'll send you a custom proposal with your pricing 
   and timeline

2. You review with your team

3. [By date], we hop on a 15-minute call to answer any final questions

4. If it looks good, we can start with your first batch of documents 
   by [start date]

Sound good?

[Confirm email address]

Great. I'm excited about this, and I really think we're going to 
help you solve the [their main pain point] issue.

Thanks for your time today!
```

## 3. OBJECTION HANDLING: PRICE CONCERN

**Script when prospect says: "Your price seems high"**

```
I hear you — and I understand price is a factor. Let me give you the full picture:

Yes, our per-document rate is [amount] vs. [competitor] at [competitor amount].

But here's what you're getting included:
- 100% accuracy guarantee (they charge rework fees if wrong)
- Guaranteed turnaround (they charge rush fees)
- Multi-state expertise (saves you legal compliance headaches)
- Account manager (dedicated support vs. calling a line)

So when you factor in [time savings] hours per month at [hourly rate], 
plus [error prevention], plus [compliance risk mitigation], 
the actual cost to you is [adjusted total] — and we're saving you [amount].

Plus, on volume, we offer tiered discounts. If you're doing [frequency], 
we can lock in [discount %] for you.

Want me to model out the numbers with different volume scenarios?
```

```

Here's what I'll tell you:

1. We've been operating for [X years] and have processed [total documents] 
   apostilles with a [error rate]% error rate.

2. We're licensed notaries in [states] and certified by [relevant authority].

3. We have references from [Company A], [Company B], [Company C] in your

4. We offer a trial program: start with 5 documents, pay standard rates, 
   zero commitment. You'll see our quality immediately.

someone else before?

[If they've used someone]: What would make you open to switching?

[If first time]: Do you want to start with a small trial batch to build 
comfort before going full volume?
```

## 5. OBJECTION HANDLING: PROCESS CHANGE RESISTANCE

**Script when prospect says: "Changing vendors is too much work"**

```
I totally get it — change is a hassle. Most of our clients felt the same way 
before switching.

But here's what makes it simple:

Step 1 of switching:
We handle the transition. You don't change a thing on your end except where you 
submit documents. Same person on your team, same approval process internally, 
just different vendor.

Step 2 of switching:
We provide step-by-step training and a dedicated account manager for the 
first month. Any hiccups, I handle them.

Step 3 of switching:
It takes about [2 weeks] for your team to adjust. After that, it's actually 
easier than your current process because [key ease factor].

Real talk: [Company A] had similar concerns. After 30 days, they told me 
they wondered why they didn't switch sooner.

Willing to give us a trial run?
```

## 6. FOLLOW-UP CALL (No Decision Yet)

**Goal:** Gentle nudge, answer remaining questions, move to "yes"

**Duration:** 5-10 minutes

---

**OPENING:**

```
[First Name], it's [Your Name] with Common Notary. Did I catch you 
at a decent time?

I wanted to follow up on the proposal I sent last [day/week]. Did you 
get a chance to review it and share with [manager/finance]?

[LISTEN]
```

**BASED ON THEIR RESPONSE:**

**If: "Haven't reviewed yet"**
```
No problem — I know you're busy. 

Just wanted to check: do you have any questions about the proposal, 
or is there anything else you'd need from us to move forward?

[Address questions if any]

When do you think you'll have a chance to review with your team? 
Can I check back with you on [specific date]?
```

**If: "Reviewed, but have questions"**
```
Great — what are the questions? Let's tackle them right now.

[Answer each thoroughly, referencing your earlier conversation]

Does that clear things up? Anything else?
```

**If: "We're going with someone else"**
```
I understand — and no hard feelings. What was the deciding factor? 
Was it price, timeline, or something else?

[LISTEN — this is valuable feedback]

If circumstances change or you want a second opinion, I'm here. 
And I'd love to stay connected — mind if I check in with you in 
[3-6 months]?
```

**If: "Yes, let's move forward"**
```
Excellent! I'm thrilled. Here's what happens next:

[Outline onboarding steps]

You're going to be happy with this — I'm confident we're going to 
solve [their main pain point] for you.

Thanks for partnering with us!
```

## PHONE SCRIPT BEST PRACTICES

### TONE & DEMEANOR:
- ✅ Conversational (not robotic)
- ✅ Confident but not pushy
- ✅ Curious (ask lots of questions)
- ✅ Empathetic (validate their challenges)
- ✅ Respectful (their time is valuable)

### PACING:
- ✅ Don't rush — let them respond fully
- ✅ Pause for 2-3 seconds after asking a question
- ✅ Don't fill silence with unnecessary talking
- ✅ Speed up slightly if they seem impatient
- ✅ Slow down if complex topic

### LISTENING:
- ✅ Take notes (if on call, tell them you're taking notes)
- ✅ Reference their words back to them
- ✅ Ask follow-up questions to show genuine interest
- ✅ Don't interrupt (wait for full response)
- ✅ Validate their concerns ("I hear you," "That makes sense")

### DO NOT:
- ❌ Pitch before understanding their needs
- ❌ Talk over them or interrupt
- ❌ Make promises you can't keep
- ❌ Slam competitor products unfairly
- ❌ Rush them toward a decision
- ❌ Call without verifying it's a good time
