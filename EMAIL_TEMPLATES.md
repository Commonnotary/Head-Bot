## GENERAL CUSTOMIZATION GUIDELINES

When using these templates:

1. **Always personalize:**
   - Use prospect's name throughout
   - Reference specific details from discovery calls
   - Mention competitor pain points they mentioned

2. **Keep subject lines short** (40-50 characters)
   - Mobile-friendly
   - Benefit-focused, not generic

3. **Use specific numbers:**
   - Instead of "save time" → "save 4 hours per month"
   - Instead of "better service" → "guaranteed 48-hour turnaround"

4. **Add proof:**
   - Testimonials from similar companies
   - Case study specifics (volume, results, timeline)
   - Statistical evidence (X% of companies experience Y)

5. **Clear call-to-action (CTA):**
   - One main CTA per email
   - Make it easy (reply, click link, call me)
   - Offer multiple ways to respond
